How to make Asian Beef Slaw

This meal has it all - beef, shredded veggies, soy sauce, hot sauce,

and lots of fresh cilantro. This dish is truly addictive.

incredients

  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced, or more to taste
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger

steps

  1. Heat canola oil in a very large skillet or wok over medium heat. Add garlic and ginger; cook and stir until fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes.

    Add ground beef; cook and stir until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.

  2. Push ground beef to

    1 side of skillet and add white cabbage, red cabbage, carrots, and red bell pepper. Mix vegetables with the beef and cook

    ,

    stirring occasionally, until vegetables are just tender, 5 to 6 minutes.
